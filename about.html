About Zen Lofi

Zen Lofi exists for one reason: to give you a quiet corner of the internet where you can actually get things done — or just breathe for a minute.

We live in a world that constantly demands our attention. Every app, every notification, every tab is fighting for a piece of you. Zen Lofi is the opposite of that. It's a place you come to subtract noise, not add it.

🎵 What We Built

Zen Lofi combines three things that help people focus: lo-fi music streams, layerable ambient sounds (rain, fire, café, ocean — mix them however you like), and a simple Pomodoro focus timer. No accounts. No tracking. No nonsense. Free forever.

🌱 The Philosophy

The best productivity tools get out of your way. You shouldn't need a tutorial to use a timer or play rain sounds. Everything on Zen Lofi is designed to be picked up in seconds and forgotten about — in the good way — while you do the actual work.

🌟 The Daily Cheer

We're big fans of The Daily Cheer — a simple dose of positivity to start your day. If Zen Lofi helps you find focus, The Daily Cheer helps you find the right mindset to use it. They go together well.
