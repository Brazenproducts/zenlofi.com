Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Your privacy matters. Zen Lofi is designed to be a simple, low-footprint tool, and we collect as little data as possible to make it work. Here's exactly what we do — and don't — do.

1. Information We Collect

Zen Lofi does not require you to create an account. We do not collect your name, email address, or any personal data by default.

The following data is stored locally in your browser using localStorage — it never leaves your device:

  • Stream preferences (likes/dislikes/weights)
  • Premium waitlist email address (if you choose to submit one)
  • Timer settings and preferences

2. Analytics & Cookies

Zen Lofi may use standard web analytics (such as Google Analytics or similar privacy-respecting tools) to understand aggregate traffic — things like which pages are visited and how often. This data is anonymized and never tied to individual identities.

Embedded YouTube players may set cookies per Google's own privacy policy. See Google's Privacy Policy for details.

3. Third-Party Services

Zen Lofi uses or links to these third-party services:

  • YouTube (Google): Lo-fi stream embeds. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Ko-fi: Voluntary donations. Subject to Ko-fi's Privacy Policy.
  • Google AdSense: Display advertising (when enabled). Subject to Google's advertising privacy policy.
  • Amazon Associates: Affiliate links in "Zen Lofi Picks." We may earn a commission if you purchase through these links at no extra cost to you.
  • SoundJay: Ambient audio files. We link directly to their CDN.

4. Advertising

Zen Lofi may display advertisements via Google AdSense. These ads may use cookies and interest-based targeting per Google's advertising policies. You can opt out of personalized ads at adssettings.google.com.

5. Affiliate Links

Some links on this site, particularly in the "Zen Lofi Picks" section, are Amazon affiliate links. We earn a small commission when you purchase through them. This has no effect on the price you pay and helps keep Zen Lofi free.

6. Children's Privacy

Zen Lofi is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review it periodically.

8. Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Reach us through our Ko-fi page.
